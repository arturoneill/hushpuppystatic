  • How does the personal errand service work?

    This is our personal, on-demand delivery service. You can place an order by calling, sending an email, or filling out the form in the "Services" section of our website. Once you let us know what you need and when, we'll get right back to you to work out the logistics. You can use our map to make sure your locations are in range, but give us a call if you're not sure. If you need us to pick up an item from a store and it is possible for you to pay ahead, we highly recommed you take care of that first. Otherwise, we can front up to $50 if necessary. We can provide call, text, or email delivery confirmation upon request.
    We have multiple options for accepting payment, including PayPal and Venmo. We take cash or card at pick-up/drop-off, and card over the phone. Payment service processing fees are applicable where they apply. No other processing fees or sales tax apply. top ^
